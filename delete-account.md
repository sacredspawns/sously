# Sously — Account deletion

**Effective date:** 2026-04-28
**Last updated:** 2026-05-14
**Publisher:** Sacred Spawns
**Contact:** [support@sacredspawns.com](mailto:support@sacredspawns.com)

This page explains how to delete your Sously account and all associated
data. The instructions apply identically to the Android and iOS versions
of Sously.

---

## In-app deletion (recommended)

The fastest, most thorough way is from inside the app:

1. Open **Sously**.
2. Tap **Settings** (gear icon) → **Account & Sync**.
3. Tap **Delete account**.
4. Read the confirmation screen carefully — it lists exactly what gets removed.
5. Type `DELETE` in the confirmation field, then tap **Permanently delete**.

This deletes your account immediately. There is no recovery window — if you
change your mind after pressing Permanently delete, you'll need to start over
with a new account.

---

## Email-channel deletion (lost device, locked out, etc.)

If you can't open the app — phone lost, account locked, signed out and forgot
the password — email **[support@sacredspawns.com](mailto:support@sacredspawns.com)**
with the subject line:

> **Sously account deletion**

Include the email address tied to your Sously account in the body. We respond
within 30 days, typically much sooner. We may ask you to verify ownership
(reply from the registered email; provide last-4 of any subscription
transaction id) before we delete to prevent malicious deletion attempts.

---

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

- Your **Sously account** in Firebase Authentication (your sign-in record)
- Your **cloud-synced library** — recipes, pantry, meal plans, shopping
  lists, templates, categories, tags, and cookbooks
- **AI-generated recipe images** stored under your account
- Your **monthly AI usage history** and quota counters
- Your **local Sously library** on the device where you trigger deletion
- Your **subscription record** in our billing provider, RevenueCat

If you're in a **shared 2-person household**: only your membership leaves
the household. Your partner keeps every recipe, pantry item, meal plan, and
shopping list. They will lose Pro access if they were relying on your
subscription, but they keep all your shared cooking data.

---

## What does NOT get deleted automatically

**Your Sously Pro subscription does not cancel automatically.** Subscriptions
live with the store you purchased through (Google or Apple), not with us — we
can't cancel them on your behalf, and account deletion does not stop renewals.
If you have an active subscription, you must cancel it separately:

- **Android (Google Play):** open the Play Store app → tap your profile photo
  → **Payments & subscriptions** → **Subscriptions** → **Sously** → **Cancel
  subscription**. Or visit
  [play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions](https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions)
  on the web.
- **iOS (App Store):** open **Settings** → tap **[your name]** at the top →
  **Subscriptions** → **Sously** → **Cancel Subscription**. (Alternatively,
  open the App Store app → tap your profile photo top-right → **Subscriptions**.)
- **Mac:** open **App Store** → click your name (bottom-left) → **Account
  Settings** → scroll to **Subscriptions** → **Manage** → **Sously** → **Cancel**.

We do not issue refunds for payments made before deletion. Cancel your
subscription **before** deleting your account if you want to avoid further
charges. On Apple, refund requests go through Apple directly at
[reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com); on Google, refund
requests go through the Play Store entry above.

## Data we retain after deletion

We retain the following anonymized records as required by law or platform
policy:

- **Crashlytics crash reports** (Firebase, default 90-day retention) —
  contains device model, OS, stack trace, but no personally-identifiable
  information from your account.
- **Aggregate usage metrics** (no per-user breakdown) — total request
  counts to our AI proxy, deidentified.

We do not retain your email, recipes, photos, or any other personal data
after deletion is complete.
