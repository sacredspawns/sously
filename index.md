# Sously

A meal-planning kitchen co-pilot.

Sously lives entirely on your phone unless you choose to sign in. Recipes,
pantry, meal plans, and shopping lists are local-first by default. Cloud
sync, household sharing, and AI features are part of the optional Sously Pro
subscription.

This site hosts the documentation Google Play, the App Store, and our users
need to make informed decisions about their data.

- **[Privacy Policy]({{ '/privacy/' | relative_url }})** — what we collect,
  where it goes, how long we keep it, and how to opt out.
- **[Terms of Use]({{ '/terms/' | relative_url }})** — the agreement that
  governs your use of Sously, including subscription terms.
- **[Account Deletion]({{ '/delete-account/' | relative_url }})** — how to
  permanently delete your Sously account and every piece of data we hold for
  you, in-app or by emailing us.

For everything else, contact **support@sacredspawns.com**.
